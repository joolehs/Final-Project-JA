Story

One Piece (Japanese: ワンピース) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. It has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine since July 22, 1997, with the chapters collected into 86 tankōbon volumes to date.

Gol · D · Roger was the only person in history to have conquered the “Grand Line” and amassed a vast fortune, the legendary treasure ONE PIECE. This earned him the title “King of the Pirates”. Wealth, Fame, Power. He obtained this and everything else the world had to offer, and his dying words drove countless souls to the seas.

“My treasure? I want you all to find it! I left everything in one place.”

These words lured men to the Grand Line, in pursuit of dreams greater than they've ever dared to imagine! The pirates fight under the banner of belief and raise their names, looking for ambition, while some seek passion, over the One Piece left by the Pirate King. In such a time full of adventure, one boy aimed for “King of the Pirates” and set sail for the Grand Line!

The StrawHat Pirates

luffy · zoro · nami · usopp · sanji · chopper · robin · franky · brook

Luffy

“I will become the Pirate King!”


Monkey D. Luffy, also known as “Straw Hat Luffy”, is the founder and captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is the first member of the crew, as well as one of its top three fighters. Luffy has eaten the Gomu Gomu no Mi, which gives the consumer's body the properties of rubber. His lifelong dream is to become the Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure left behind by the late Pirate King, Gol D. Roger. He believes that being Pirate King means having the most freedom in the world.

Wanted: 500,000,000 Beli

Zoro

“If you can't even protect your captain,
then your ambition is worthless.”


Roronoa Zoro, also known as “Pirate Hunter Zoro”, is a former bounty hunter. He was the first member to join the Straw Hat Pirates, and, to date, is considered the largest threat and most dangerous member in the crew after Luffy. His fame as a master swordsman and his great strength, along with the actions of his captain, sometimes led others to believe he was the true captain of the crew. He is one of the top three fighters in the crew, and his dream is to become the greatest swordsman in the world.

Wanted: 320,000,000 Beli

Nami

“What good is treasure if I'm alone?”


Nami, also known as “Cat Burglar Nami”, is the navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates. Nami’s childhood was ruined by the fiendish fishman Arlong. She harbored a hatred for pirates until she fell in with Luffy and the Straw Hats. She is able to use her three sectioned staff and use her climate skills to create powerful attacks. She has an obsession of obtaining money and dreams of drawing a map of the entire world.

Wanted: 66,000,000 Beli

Usopp

“There comes a time when a man will not run away from a fight, that time is when his nakama's dreams are being laughed at.”


Usopp, also known as “God Usopp”, is the sniper of the Straw Hat Pirates, and the former captain of the Usopp Pirates. He is a cowardly marksman with a talent for telling tall tales, but when his friends need him most, he always comes up big! During the Post-Enies Lobby Arc, he went by an alias "Sogeking". His dream during this journey is to become a brave warrior of the sea, just like his father before him.

Wanted: 200,000,000 Beli

Sanji

“Cooking is a gift from God.
Spices are a gift from the devil.
Looks like it was too spicy for you.”


Vinsmoke Sanji, also known as “Black Leg Sanji”, is the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates, as well as the former sous chef of the Baratie. He has a soft spot for beautiful women, but don't cross him though. He learned to fight at the feet of Red-Foot Zeff, so he knows a thing or two about giving bad guys the boot. He is one of the Monster Trio in the crew, alongside Luffy and Zoro. His dream is to find the rumored chef's paradise, All Blue, which is where East, West, North, and South Blue meet, along with their wildlife.

Wanted: 177,000,000 Beli

Chopper

“If I can be of any help,
I'll even become a real monster.”


Tony Tony Chopper, also known as “Cotton Candy Lover Chopper”, is the doctor of the Straw Hat Pirates. Chopper is a reindeer that ate the Hito Hito no Mi which is a devil fruit that allows its user to transform into a human hybrid or a human at will. He came from Drum Island where he learned how to be a doctor, which also makes him the only member of the Straw Hats who was born on the Grand Line. He is also the youngest member on board. Chopper's dream is to become a great doctor that can cure any disease.

Wanted: 100 Beli

Robin

“I want to live!”


Nico Robin, also known as “Devil Child” and the “Light of the Revolution”, is the archaeologist of the Straw Hat Pirates. She joined the Straw Hats at the tail end of their adventure in Alabasta. She ate the Hana Hana no Mi which allows the user to replicate and sprout pieces of their body from the surface of any object or living thing. She carries the scars of a dark and tragic past, but she dreams of finding the Rio Poneglyph, which tells the True History of the One Piece world.

Wanted: 130,000,000 Beli

Franky

“No matter what kind of weapon you hold... Just existing can't be a crime!”


Franky, also known as “Iron Man Franky”, is the shipwright for the Straw Hat Pirates. He was originally the leader of the Franky Family, a group of ship dismantlers. He is a blue-haired, speedo-clad, cola-powered cyborg who is full of surprises! He initially burst on the scene as an enemy during the Water 7 arc, but his cool attitude and awesome array of gadgetry quickly wins over the Straw Hats. He's one heck of a shipwright, a ferocious fighter, and the guardian of a very deadly secret! Franky decided to join the Straw Hats to fulfill his dream of creating and riding a ship capable of circumnavigating the world, the Thousand Sunny.

Wanted: 94,000,000 Beli

Brook

“Die and Leave a grudge? That's absurd! After Death, nothing remains.”


Brook, also known as “Soul King Brook”, is the musician of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is a skeleton that the Straw Hats found on board of a ghost ship after drifting into the Florian Triangle. He is a devil fruit user who ate the Yomi Yomi no Mi, and is undead, having been brought back into a pseudo-immortal state because of its powers. He is the oldest and the tallest member on board. He is also one of the two swordsmen on board their current ship. Brook's dream is to return to Laboon, with the promise to return after crossing the Grand Line, and deliver their final song recorded in a Tone Dial before his former crew's death.

Wanted: 83,000,000 Beli
